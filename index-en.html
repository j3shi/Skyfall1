SKYFALL 1 LEVI

is a ski-out & putt-in cottage. It's only 100 m to the ski lift (slope 6 base station) and 100 m to the golf course. You don’t necessarily need a car – the ski bus runs just 100 m away all day long.

The cottage is ideal for 2–3 families thanks to its location and layout. Vacationing and moving around is very easy and convenient. Welcome to explore!

The downstairs has 4 bedrooms, a renovated sauna, and a bathroom with two showers. Two bedrooms have wide double beds, and the other two have 2x80 cm beds plus a 160x210 cm loft bed above each that comfortably sleeps 2 people. There’s also a drying cabinet in the entry and 2 toilets.

Upstairs you'll find a spacious living room with stunning views of the slope in front of the massive stone fireplace. The space is open and flows into the kitchen and dining area, which has a 14-person dining table. There’s also a sleeping loft with two 80 cm mattresses.

Basic info: • 12–14 guests • Total area 120 m² • Nearest ski lift 100 m • Levi center 4.5 km • Ski trail ~50 m • Snowmobile route 50 m • Airport 16 km • Ski bus 100 m
